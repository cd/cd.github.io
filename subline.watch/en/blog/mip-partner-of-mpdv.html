Official MIP Partner of MPDV

SubLine Solutions is now an official MIP partner of MPDV, the market leader for IT solutions in the manufacturing sector. SubLine Watch is therefore part of the MPDV ecosystem as a so-called Manufacturing App (mApp).

A strong partner has been found in MPDV, which promises new marketing impulses and strengthens trust in the smartwatch solution.

Users of the Manufacturing Integration Platform (MIP) who opt for SubLine Watch benefit from a more easier commissioning thanks to an existing MQTT broker and a semantic data structure. A cross-mApp data access also makes it possible to easily use data from other mApps for SubLine Watch to generate new use cases.

Joint ventures - also with other MIP partners - ensure a good starting point for future projects.
