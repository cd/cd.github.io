SubLine Watch Newly Presented

Information on changes to the external presentation of SubLine Watch and the company behind it - SubLine Solutions.

SubLine Solutions as an End-2-End System Integrator

SubLine Solutions now presents itself more as a supplier of individual end-to-end solutions and less as a pure manufacturer of standard software. Practice has shown that companies need exactly that: Someone who takes them by the hand and implements smartwatch solutions ready for operation in their individual system landscape and according to their requirements.

Web Presence With a New Look

In order to be able to present more information more clearly, the website was redesigned. The landing page became a classic multisite. The outdated product video was removed and will soon be replaced by a new video.

Intralogistics More in Focus

The possible applications of SubLine Watch are various and not limited to operation in production plants. In particular, logistics in manufacturing companies are frequently requested requirements. This sector is therefore now more clearly listed on the website as a possible use case.
